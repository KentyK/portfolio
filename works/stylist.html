2015/01 ~ 2015/10

Stylist

Role: UI Design / Software Engineer
Language: Swift
Awards: Finalist (out of 1300) and awarded “Hikari TV Award, Teens Apps Award 2014

“Stylist” is a wardrobe organizer created to assist outfit planning. Users take pictures of their clothes and “Stylist” automatically tags and saves the upload photos to its respective categories using Deep Learning. The split screen top-bottom mode (FIT) allows users to swipe and visualize combinations. This app also suggests the best outfit based on the weather for the day. The significant feature that makes “Stylist” unique is the iBeacon that is installed, which enables one way communication between stores and users within 50 meters distance. By transmitting targeted advertisement and special offers, stores can boost their sales while users can get the best deal.
