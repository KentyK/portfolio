Disney Codeillusion

Role: Localization / Curriculum / LX (Puzzles)
Created by Life is Tech USA, Inc.

“Disney Codeillusion” is a beginner friendly self instructional online programming tutorial co developed with Disney.
Effective story telling techniques engage students to learn the key elements of programming languages and the fundamentals of coding. Utilizing multiple programming languages and journeying theme based activities such as game, media art, and web design, this curriculum aims to nurture a deeper understanding of programming and to enhance student’s motivation and creativity.
I was involved in product development especially the storytelling and narrative strategies reporting to Creative Manager and CEO.
